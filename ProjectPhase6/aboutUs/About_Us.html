About US

While desperately trying not to fail or succumb to senioritis/sophmoritis, our group thought back to being confused little orange cows. We wanted an informal hub of information to get the real scoop on UT. Freshmen want to know if the $8 for dining halls is worth it or should they settle for a Jendy's run, where to get resources, and where's the quietest restroom on campus. With the recent digitization of our lives, some students may be missing the longhorn experience, which we hope to provide for.

For New Horns, or new longhorns, without the honor of upperclassmen friends or siblings, we offer a community of just that. Old Horns. Rickety, crotchety, tired upperclassmen who would like to bestow all of their wisdom before dumping it down the drain like other specific informations our burned out braincells can take.
